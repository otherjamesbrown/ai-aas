# Billing Configuration

## Overview

This guide covers configuring billing settings, payment preferences, and invoice settings.

## Billing Settings

### Billing Information

- **Billing Contact**: Primary billing contact
- **Billing Address**: Billing address
- **Tax Information**: Tax ID and details
- **Payment Terms**: Payment terms and conditions

### Invoice Settings

- **Invoice Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly, annually
- **Invoice Format**: PDF, email, portal
- **Invoice Delivery**: Email, portal, mail
- **Invoice Numbering**: Custom numbering scheme
