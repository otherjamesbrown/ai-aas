# Invoice Management

## Overview

This guide covers managing invoices, processing payments, and handling billing inquiries.

## Invoice Overview

### Invoice Types

- **Monthly Invoices**: Regular monthly billing
- **Usage-Based Invoices**: Based on actual usage
- **Prepaid Invoices**: Advance payment invoices
- **Adjustment Invoices**: Corrections and adjustments

### Invoice Components

- Service charges
- Usage charges
- Taxes and fees
- Payment terms
