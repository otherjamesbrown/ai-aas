# Payment Processing

## Overview

This guide covers processing payments, managing payment methods, and handling payment issues.

## Payment Methods

### Supported Methods

- **Credit Card**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Bank Transfer**: ACH, wire transfer
- **Invoice Payment**: Net terms payment
- **Prepaid Credits**: Advance payment

### Adding Payment Methods

1. Navigate to Billing section
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. Enter payment details
4. Verify payment method
5. Set as default (optional)

## Payment Processing

### Processing Payments

- Process automatic payments
- Process manual payments
- Handle payment failures
- Retry failed payments

### Payment Status

- **Pending**: Payment processing
- **Completed**: Payment successful
- **Failed**: Payment failed
- **Refunded**: Payment refunded

## Payment Issues

### Payment Failures

- Identify failure reason
- Update payment method
- Retry payment
- Contact support if needed

### Refunds

- Process refunds
- Issue credit memos
- Update records
- Notify customers
