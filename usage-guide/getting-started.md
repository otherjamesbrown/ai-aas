# Getting Started

## Overview

Welcome to the AIaaS platform! This guide will help you get started based on your role.

## First Steps

### 1. Access the Platform

- Contact your organization administrator for access
- Receive invitation email
- Accept invitation and set up account
- Complete profile setup

### 2. Understand Your Role

- Review your persona guide:
  - [Architects](./architect/README.md)
  - [Security](./security/README.md)
  - [System Administrators](./system-admin/README.md)
  - [Operations](./operations/README.md)
  - [Organization Administrators](./org-admin/README.md)
  - [Finance](./finance/README.md)
  - [Developers](./developer/README.md)
  - [Managers](./manager/README.md)
  - [Analysts](./analyst/README.md)

### 3. Complete Initial Setup

- Set up authentication (MFA recommended)
- Review organization settings
- Understand available features
- Configure preferences
