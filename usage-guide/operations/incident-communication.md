# Communication During Incidents

## Overview

This guide covers communication procedures during incidents, including stakeholder notification and status updates.

## Communication Channels

### Internal Communication

- **Slack/Teams**: Real-time team communication
- **Incident Management Tool**: Centralized incident tracking
- **Email**: Formal notifications
- **Phone**: Critical escalations

### External Communication

- **Status Page**: Public status updates
- **Email**: Customer notifications
- **Support Portal**: Customer inquiries
- **Social Media**: Public announcements (if needed)

## Communication Timeline

### Initial Notification

- **Immediate**: Notify incident response team
- **Within 15 minutes**: Update status page
- **Within 30 minutes**: Notify stakeholders
- **As needed**: Customer notifications

### Regular Updates

- **Every 30 minutes**: Update status page
- **Every hour**: Internal status update
- **As needed**: Customer updates for critical incidents

### Resolution Communication

- **Immediate**: Notify team of resolution
- **Within 15 minutes**: Update status page
- **Within 1 hour**: Post-incident summary

## Status Page Updates

### Status Page Content

- Current status (operational, degraded, down)
- Affected services
- Impact description
- Estimated resolution time
- Updates timeline

### Update Frequency

- Initial update within 15 minutes
- Updates every 30 minutes during incident
- Final update upon resolution

## Customer Communication

### When to Notify Customers

- Service outage affecting customers
- Data breach or security incident
- Extended maintenance window
- Significant performance degradation

### Communication Content

- Incident description
- Impact assessment
- Expected resolution time
- Workarounds (if available)
- Contact information

## Stakeholder Communication

### Internal Stakeholders

- Engineering leadership
- Product management
- Customer success
- Executive team (for critical incidents)

### Communication Content

- Incident summary
- Current status
- Impact assessment
- Resolution plan
- Timeline updates

## Post-Incident Communication

### Incident Report

- Incident summary
- Timeline of events
- Root cause analysis
- Impact assessment
- Remediation steps
- Prevention measures

### Customer Follow-up

- Apology for impact
- Explanation of incident
- Steps taken to prevent recurrence
- Compensation (if applicable)

## Best Practices

### Communication Principles

- Be transparent and honest
- Provide regular updates
- Use clear, non-technical language
- Acknowledge customer impact
- Provide actionable information

### Avoid

- Speculation about root cause
- Blaming others
- Making promises you can't keep
- Delaying communication
- Using technical jargon
